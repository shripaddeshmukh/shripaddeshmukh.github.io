About Me

I am originally from Solapur, MH and currently reside in New Delhi. I hold B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. After graduating in 2020, I have been working at Adobe.

Here at Adobe, I work as a machine learning research associate since early 2022. I have been working on problems in reinforcement learning (RL) and document analysis. Before that, I used to work in an ML engineering role where my task was to deploy and maintain ML models for Adobe's Form Conversion service. My current interest lies in researching ways to build deployable RL systems - ensuring the quality of performance, safety during execution, and human interpretability. I have had the fortune to work with Georgios Theocharous and Prof. Nan Jiang on a problem in RL deployability.

In my leisure time, I like to read books, paint, and explore Delhi. My reading list can be found here. Some of my paintings can be found here. And as for my adventurous explorations in Delhi, after coming here in May'22 (before that I was working from home), I have visited National Museum, India Gate, Jama Masjid and Connaught Place, and my wishlist is still growing. (Let me know if you too have any suggestions!)
